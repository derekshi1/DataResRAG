SPECIAL UCLA GRADES

  • J = Delayed Grade
  • R = Temporary Grade - Course added retroactively
  • L = Temporary Grade - Study List filed late
  • I = Incomplete
  • MG = Multiple-course Grade
  • MT = Multiple-term Course
  • DR = Deferred Report
  • NR = No Report received from Instructor
  • AP = Indicates Advanced Placement Course
  • IB = Indicates International Baccalaureate
  • SAT2 = Indicates SAT II Exam

TRANSFER GRADES

  • Tnn = Transfer grade (e.g., TA, TB, TC+)
  • TBX = Grade between A- & B+
  • TCX = Grade between B- & C+
  • TDX = Grade between C- & D+
  • TIF = Incomplete Fail
  • TRPT = Course Repeated

SPECIAL CHARACTERS

  • SQ = Course out of sequence
  • OK = Requirement complete
  • OR = Indicates choice of requirements
  • IP = In Progress course used in the requirement
  • OPT = Optional requirement
  • OKOPT = Optional requirement completed
